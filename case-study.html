The Challenge: "It's a Full-Time Job"

The Context: Our B2B platform serves enterprise clients with 500+ employees.

The Pain Point: The old system required manual setup for every single user. Customer Success Managers (CSMs) were spending hours manually inputting emails and toggling 20+ permission checkboxes for each employee.

  • CSMs acted as "Data Entry" clerks.
  • Admins were terrified of the "Delete" button (fear of data loss).
  • Permissions were granular and incomprehensible (e.g., "Can view metadata" vs "Can edit metadata").

Clarifying Permissions (RBAC)

Research showed Admins didn't care about "Edit Metadata". They cared about "Can this intern break the system?". I grouped 50+ granular permissions into 4 clear Roles: Owner, Admin, Editor, Viewer.
